Meet Laurie Meinhold

MTNA nationally certified piano teacher in Aliso Viejo.

Laurie earned her Bachelor of Music in piano performance (with honors) from California State University, Fullerton and a Master of Arts in Music from Claremont Graduate University. She has performed as a classical soloist, school accompanist, and church musician, and serves as an adjudicator for MTAC, CAPMT, and the National Guild of Piano Teachers.

Students of every ability — gifted learners, beginners to advanced — find a thoughtful, encouraging path toward their goals.

  • • Past president, MTAC Orange Coast Cities branch
  • • Training in the Alexander Technique with a focus on easeful playing
  • • Students earn festival honors and continue into collegiate studies
